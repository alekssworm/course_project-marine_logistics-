ще таке Ferrum Logistica ?

Ferrum Logistica – is an international company specializing in ship transportation of goods. The company was founded several years ago, and during this time it managed to establish itself as a reliable partner for its customers. 

Ferrum Logistica provides cargo transportation services between ports, as well as cargo warehousing and customs clearance services.

Why should you choose us?

The Ferrum Logistica team consists of highly qualified shipping and logistics professionals. Each employee of the company has the necessary knowledge and experience to ensure the smooth operation of the company and ensure the timely delivery of goods. 

Our mission is to ensure safe and fast delivery of goods for our customers. We strive to provide services at the highest level, taking into account the individual needs of each client. Ferrum Logistica is ready to become your reliable partner in the field of shipping and logistics!

Our Transportation is the Guarantor of Safety

Control over the movement of cargo on ships is one of the main aspects of ensuring security. Our company ensures the safety of cargo by using high-tech methods of tracking and monitoring our vessels, such as GPS, AIS, satellite communication and monitoring systems. 

This allows us to know exactly the state and condition of our vessels, as well as the conditions of cargo transportation. Thanks to these measures, we can maximally secure the goods during their transportation and prevent possible risks and losses.
